NEW YORK

Food & Drinks

It wouldn’t be new york without a bagel, of course and whilst locals recommend Russ & Daughters or Murray’s Bagels, you won’t be short of places to find one!

To satisfy your sweet tooth head to Magnolia’s Bakery or Milk bar and you won’t be disappointed and don’t forget to grab a New York Cheesecake! When you want to splurge, head to the Michelin starred Blue Hill or would you rather, save your coins and grab a bite from a food truck - just like the locals do it.

Places to see

New York is home to some of the most beautiful architecture in the world, just visit Brooklyn Bridge or the Empire State Building and see what the world’s been talking about! If you’re still not impressed, visit the One Word Observatory and the view of the whole city will take your breath way.

Get lost on the subway? Just catch a train to Grand Central, which is a destination in and of itself and place to stop and people watch. Of course, a trip to New York isn’t complete without seeing the Statue of Liberty, just don’t forget your flats for the steps!

Things to do

New York is a city for everyone! You haven’t been to a seaside town until you’ve been to Coney Island. Reminiscent of everyone’s childhood and full of charm, it’s bound to be a great day out for anyone. Theatre enthusiasts can grab a bargain from the infamous TKTS booth under the red steps in Duffy Square then catch a show on broadway for half the price!

Even if you’re not an art buff, you have to visit a museum in New York - you’re spoilt for choice with the Museum of Modern Art, the Guggenheim and the Metropolitan Museum of Art right on your doorstep. Escape the ram-packed side walks to some greenery in Central or Prospect Park. The city never sleeps so neither should you!
