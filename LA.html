LOS ANGELES

Here is a suggestion based on your preferences!

Food & Drinks

Every major metropolitan city has food to satisfy any palate and LA is no exception. Head to Fairfax Avenue for some of the best brunch spots or Downtown for the likes of Pizza from Pizzana or tacos from Sonoratown.When you’re after something light, then why not splurge on sushi at the Nozawa Bar in Beverley Hills ? Or, if you’re more sweet than salty, then you won’t be dissappointed by a pastry from Republique or one of the innovative creations from The Pie Hole. Don’t just head to the big names though. Why not tuck yourself away somewhere intimate and explore authentic dishes in little Tokyo or Chinatown? Beyond the restaurants, LA has one of the world’s best farmers’ markets, where you can taste the very best fresh produce. You won’t be able to leave a bite behind!

Places to see

LA is the ideal destination for entertainment fans: from Hollywood Boulevard’s ‘Walk of Fame’, the Warner Brothers’ Studios or the famous Hollywood Sign, the city is not short of sights for entertainment fans. LA is not just the city of glamour and glitz though, if you’re after the extraordinary, head to the La Brea Tar Mits, which are a conundrum for us all or the California Science Center to see a real-life space shuttle. Venice Broadwark is like a world of its own, packed in every inch with artists, skaters, musicians and dancers - it’s by far the coolest place on the West Coast.Make sure you spend an evening in the Griffith Observatory and catch stunning views of the whole city

Things to do

LA is the ideal destination for entertainment fans: from Hollywood Boulevard’s ‘Walk of Fame’, the Warner Brothers’ Studios or the famous Hollywood Sign, the city is not short of sights for entertainment fans. LA is not just the city of glamour and glitz though, if you’re more of a sports kind of person, grab the opportunity to catch a Dodgers or Lakers game or, if you’re short of things to do, take advantage of the city’s beautiful weather and spend some time outdoors in Griffith Park or Santa Monica Pier.
